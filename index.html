Positional Accuracy of NV Centers: σ Scaling from Single Emitter to the General Case
Builds up from the simplest single-NV baseline through the two-emitter Rayleigh curse, the combinatorial catastrophe of M emitters, and the fully general unknown-M problem. Ends with how superresolution rescues the estimation problem by enforcing Meff ≤ 1.

Magnetic Field Misalignment in NV Center Experiments
Basis transformations, initialization fidelity, and readout physics under a misaligned magnetic field. Covers first-order perturbation theory for the mixed eigenstates, the π-pulse error, the full PL measurement operator, and the crossover to the level anti-crossing at ~102 mT.
